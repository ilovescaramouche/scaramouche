About Me

Hi! I am Allison Ciera Pineda, a proud student of Las Pinas City National Science High School. As of now the time when I am writing this, I am 14 years old. I will be turning 15 in less than a month, on November 7. Despite the fact that I was born on November 7, 2010; I often tell people I was born on January 3, 2010. I choose to tell people that because my favourite character was born on that date. In order to further be closer to him, I often modify my unimportant personal details to be like his.

Personal Details

Circle of Friends

Over the course of my entire life, I've met numerous people. Despite knowing several people, I would only call a few of them my true friends. As of now, I would claim to have only 2 friends that I communicate with regularly. One of them attends the same school I do, whereas the other one, my old friend of 5 years, attends Divine Light Academy.

My Family

I reside in the same home as two of my family members: My mother and my aunt. I do not have a father as he left when I was but a wee fetus. Many people claim that I'm a carbon copy of my mother. We're both reclusive neets who have an obsession with books, anime, and games. My aunt, on the other hand, shares my love for fashion. I often get my hand-me-downs from her, as I do not have any siblings.

Additional Information

I have yet to achieve anything worthwhile or phrase-worthy in my life. I'm average with no prospects in life, and I live in a mundane routine. I would describe myself as many things, but I believe I center myself most as being Scaramouche's number 1 fan!

7- Blessedness (9/26/23)

His eyes are dark and blue
His names are a lot, not a few
His tongue is sharp and cold
His actions, dark and bold

Such a man so unliked
His eyes, always full of spite
The man who often showed delight in other people's plight
taken down by a traveler so full of might

three betrayals that harmed his soul
He lost his joy and his control
The first was one from his creator
The man was cast away to wither

The second pain was from a bond stronger than blood
A single-life lost and the other turned bad
The third was one I wish were kind
The man watched his own friend die
A promise went void as he saw it a mere lie

All the pains were not for naught
He was not kind but he grew a lot
Eventually, the man learned the truth of the betrayals
Each being the result of love not close to fragile

The first was from his mother, who could not watch him suffer
The second from one like his father, who gave his life for the other
The last was a promise broken fast, caused by a life ended by a mishap
All those times he felt unkind, were caused by his blind-sighted mind

The man cried with his newfound life
by truths he found under disguised lies
He erased his misdeeds from the world
His past buried in a mind not so pure
